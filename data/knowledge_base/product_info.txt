=== Product Care Instructions / 产品保养说明 ===

Electronics:
- Keep away from water and extreme temperatures
- Clean with microfiber cloth only
- Update software regularly
- Store in protective cases when not in use

Clothing:
- Follow care label instructions
- Wash similar colors together
- Air dry delicate items
- Iron on appropriate temperature settings

Furniture:
- Dust regularly with soft cloth
- Avoid direct sunlight to prevent fading
- Tighten screws and bolts periodically
- Use coasters and placemats to protect surfaces
